Hotel Management System

1. The system should support the booking of different room types like standard, deluxe e.t.c

2. Guest should be able to search the room inventory and book any available room

3. The system should be able to retrieve information such as who booked a particular room or what rooms were booked by
a specific customer

4. The system should allow customer to cancel their booking and provide them with a full refund if the cancellation
occurs 24 hours of the check in date

5. The system should be able to send notifications whenever the booking is nearing the check in or check out date

6. The system should maintain a room housekeeping log to keep track of all housekeeping tasks

7. Any customer should be able to add room service and food items

8. The Customers should be able to pay their bills through credit card, check or cash

9. Admin should be able to add delete and edit a room

10. Receptionist should be able to check in / check out a guest
